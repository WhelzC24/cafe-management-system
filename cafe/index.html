Our Story

A Café Born from Passion

In 2026, we opened Cozy Corner Café with a simple belief: great coffee should be paired with genuine warmth. We started as a small corner shop with two espresso machines and a dream.

Today, we're a beloved community hub where regulars know each other by name, first-time visitors feel like family, and every cup is crafted by baristas who genuinely love their craft.

🌍

Ethically Sourced

Direct-trade beans from sustainable farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala.

Scratch Kitchen
